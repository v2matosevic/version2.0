## The Challenge

Villa Nadja and Villa Tamaris are two premium rental villas in Bibinje near Zadar, Croatia — each with private heated pools, rooftop jacuzzis, saunas, and 3 bedrooms with en-suite bathrooms. The owners needed a website that could showcase both properties with the visual polish of a luxury hospitality brand while keeping the user experience simple and intuitive — guests should be able to see the villas, understand what's included, explore the area, and make a booking inquiry in minutes.

The site needed to work in two languages (English and Croatian) with full SEO optimization, and give the owners a way to manage inquiries directly rather than relying entirely on third-party platforms.

## The Approach

We designed and built the entire site from scratch, including on-site photography, with a focus on clean, modern aesthetics inspired by the best in hospitality UX.

### Airbnb-Inspired Clean Design
DM Serif Display headings paired with Open Sans body text on a warm cream palette with coral (#D90700) accents. The design is intentionally restrained — generous white space, large photography, clear hierarchy. The homepage opens with a dramatic hero split between both villas, immediately letting visitors choose their property.

Every element serves a purpose: amenity icons, guest capacity badges, star ratings from real reviews. No decorative bloat.

### Individual Villa Pages
Each villa gets a dedicated page with:
- **Hero gallery** with professional interior and exterior photography
- **Highlights strip** — private heated pool, rooftop jacuzzi, poolside/indoor sauna, PlayStation 5, private parking — displayed as scannable icon badges
- **Booking inquiry form** — built directly into the villa page, visitors can select dates and send a reservation request without leaving
- **Detailed description** — about the property, what's included, seasonal availability (mid-May to end of October)
- **Google Maps integration** — embedded interactive map showing the exact location
- **House rules** — check-in/check-out times, deposit policy, cancellation terms, pet and smoking policies
- **FAQ section** — common questions answered directly on the page

### Interactive Photo Gallery
A filterable gallery page with 24+ professional photos across both villas — interiors, pool areas, terraces, bedrooms, kitchens, aerial views. Clean grid layout with lightbox viewing.

### Attractions Guide
A dedicated page showcasing what's nearby: Old Town Zadar, the Sea Organ, Dalmaland, Kornati National Park, Paklenica National Park. Positioned as a trip-planning resource that adds value beyond just the accommodation listing.

### Guest Testimonials
Real 5-star reviews from guests across Europe (France, Austria, Germany) displayed prominently on the homepage, building trust through social proof.

### Admin Backend
An admin panel where the owners can view visitor statistics and make simple edits to the villa listings — update descriptions, swap photos, adjust availability details — without needing developer involvement.

### Two-Language SEO
Full English and Croatian versions with proper hreflang tags, localized URLs, and SEO-optimized meta descriptions for both markets.

## The Outcome

A polished, professional rental website that gives Villa Nadja & Tamaris a direct booking presence independent of Airbnb and Booking.com. The site builds brand credibility with premium photography, clear information architecture, and a booking flow that keeps the guest experience smooth from first click to inquiry.

The admin backend means the owners stay in control of their content and can track how the site performs, while the attractions page and detailed villa information give the site SEO value beyond just the property name — capturing "things to do near Zadar" and "villas with pool Bibinje" search traffic that pure listing platforms can't match.
