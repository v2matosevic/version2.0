## The Challenge

Village Homes Drage sells and installs premium mobile homes on Croatia's Adriatic coast. They needed more than a brochure website — they needed a platform that could showcase 8+ mobile home models from two manufacturers, let visitors explore camp locations with available plots, compare models side by side, and guide undecided buyers toward the right choice. All of this in four languages (English, Croatian, German, Slovenian) with proper SEO for each, and with a backend that a non-technical team could manage independently.

The site also had to feel premium. These aren't cheap caravans — they're fully furnished Mediterranean homes ranging from 24m² to 48m², positioned as lifestyle investments on the Croatian coast.

## The Approach

We handled everything from scratch: design, development, photography (both aerial drone shots and on-site product photography), and a custom CMS for ongoing content management.

### Premium Design, Fast Performance
A clean, modern aesthetic with a blue-to-navy gradient palette and generous white space. Aerial coastal photography sets the mood. Despite the rich imagery and animations, the site is highly optimized for fast loading — performance was a non-negotiable requirement.

### Interactive Camp Plot Maps
The standout feature. For each camp, the admin uploads a drone or satellite photo as a base layer, then places interactive markers directly on the image to represent individual plots. Each marker carries a status: available, sold, reserved, or coming soon. Visitors can click any marker to see details and contact the team directly about that specific plot. It turns a static camp overview into a living, interactive sales tool.

### Model Comparison Tool
Visitors can select 2-3 mobile home models and compare them side by side across specifications: size, bedrooms, bathrooms, guest capacity, brand, and features. This eliminates the back-and-forth of switching between model pages and helps buyers make confident decisions.

### Home Finder Quiz
A custom-built questionnaire of 5-6 questions that recommends the ideal mobile home model based on the visitor's answers — budget, family size, usage patterns, preferences. Built from scratch and tailored specifically to the models available on the site.

### Four-Language SEO
Every page is fully translated and SEO-optimized in English, Croatian, German, and Slovenian — the primary markets for Adriatic coastal property buyers. Proper hreflang tags, localized meta descriptions, and language-specific URL structures.

### Content Architecture
- **Models section** — 8 models across Vitolina and Hosekra brands, each with detailed specs, photo galleries, floor plans, and features
- **Camps section** — Camp profiles with amenities, photo galleries, and the interactive plot map
- **6-step process timeline** — Visual storytelling from home selection to key handover
- **Investment section** — Rental income positioning with self-managed vs. professional management options
- **News/blog** — Company updates and market insights
- **FAQ** — 8 common questions with expandable answers

### Custom CMS
The admin backend lets the team manage everything: create and edit blog posts, update camp information and plot availability on the interactive maps, manage model listings, upload photos, and track website visitor statistics. No developer needed for day-to-day operations.

## The Outcome

A platform that elevates Village Homes Drage from a local mobile home dealer to a premium coastal lifestyle brand. The site guides visitors through a clear journey — from discovering models to exploring camps to comparing options to making contact — with every step designed to build confidence and reduce friction.

The combination of interactive plot maps, the comparison tool, and the home finder quiz gives visitors tools that most competitors in this space simply don't offer. And with four-language SEO, the site reaches buyers across Central and Southern Europe where demand for Croatian coastal property is strongest.
