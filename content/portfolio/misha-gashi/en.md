## The Challenge

Miša Gaši — holistic wellness practitioner, life coach, crystal therapist, and spiritual guide — had outgrown the limitations of off-the-shelf website builders. She needed a platform that could unify her entire business under one roof: a public-facing website that reflected the premium, luxury quality of her brand, an e-commerce store for both physical and digital products, and a secure portal where clients could access purchased courses, communicate directly with her, and track their personal transformation journey.

The platform had to feel as intentional and elevated as the transformation work Miša does with her clients — no generic templates, no compromises.

## The Approach

We designed and built the entire platform from scratch as a three-part system, all connected under a single cohesive experience:

### 1. The Public Website
A premium, light-aesthetic design built around Cormorant serif headlines and gold (#C9A227) accents — luxury without excess. The homepage orchestrates 10 distinct sections that guide visitors through a narrative: identifying their pain points, discovering Miša's approach, exploring services, and taking action. Every animation, gradient, and transition serves the storytelling.

Key pages include a services showcase with detailed pricing, an About page presenting Miša's six professional certifications, an education hub, blog with featured article layout, and a contact system.

### 2. The Webshop — "Magic Garden"
An integrated e-commerce experience powered by Stripe, selling both physical products (handmade linen dresses at €280, motivational posters) and digital products (online courses, exercise packages). Features category filtering, price filtering, sorting, shopping cart, and secure checkout. The moment a digital product is purchased, it becomes immediately accessible in the user's portal — no manual provisioning needed.

### 3. The Backend Portal
The crown jewel of the project. A custom-built portal serving two distinct roles:

**For users:**
- **Course dashboard** — Access purchased courses with video lessons delivered via YouTube, Vimeo, or direct uploads. Track lesson progress, complete quizzes, and earn downloadable certificates.
- **Daily journaling** — A wellness tracking system where users log which courses they completed, what exercises they did, how they feel, their motivation level, and free-form reflections. A calendar view lets them track their transformation over weeks and months.
- **Real-time messaging** — Direct chat with Miša. Users send messages, Miša responds from her admin panel — no email middleman, no third-party tools.
- **Order history** — Full purchase records and access to all bought products.

**For admin (Miša):**
- **Full custom CMS** — Create, edit, and delete blog posts. Add and manage webshop products. Build multi-lesson courses with video from any source. No WordPress bloat, no unnecessary features — only what she needs.
- **User management** — Approve or delete accounts, grant or revoke course access per user, view user activity and journaling progress, manage all orders.
- **Direct messaging** — Respond to client messages in real-time from the same portal she uses to manage everything else.

The philosophy: a streamlined, purpose-built alternative to WordPress + WooCommerce. Less bloat, more clarity. Every screen contains exactly what the user needs and nothing they don't.

## The Outcome

The result is a unified platform that matches the premium quality of Miša's brand. Three interconnected systems — public site, webshop, and portal — that feel like one seamless experience. The website has received praise from visitors and peers for its stunning design, smooth animations, and the cohesive way it brings together services, commerce, and education under a single domain.

The platform gives Miša full independence to manage her entire digital business: publishing content, selling products, delivering courses, and connecting with clients — all from one place she controls completely. It's more optimized, more focused, and more intuitive than any off-the-shelf solution could be — because it was built specifically for her.
