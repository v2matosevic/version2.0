## The Challenge

SIMA Büro- & Sprachservice is a bilingual office and translation service based in Mattighofen, Austria, bridging Hungarian and German for individuals and businesses. The founder needed a website that communicates professionalism and trustworthiness instantly — clients are coming for help navigating government offices, legal paperwork, and financial processes, so the site had to feel reliable, clear, and easy to act on.

No complexity needed. Just a clean, modern site that shows what SIMA does, establishes credibility, and makes it effortless to get in touch. In two languages, properly SEO optimized.

## The Approach

We built a hand-crafted one-page website from scratch — HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, no framework overhead. Every section serves a clear purpose in the visitor's decision journey.

### Clean, Trust-Building Design
A black, white, and coral red (#D90700) palette — professional without being cold. The hero immediately establishes the value proposition: "Your Office & Translation Service for Hungarian & German." Professional photography of the consultant creates a personal connection. Stats (10+ years experience, 120+ partner institutions) build credibility above the fold.

### Service Showcase
Eight services displayed as clean, scannable cards — translation & interpretation, government office accompaniment, application assistance, vehicle registration, real estate purchase support, correspondence handling, office administration, and personal consulting. Each card has a clear headline and concise description. No jargon, no clutter.

### Four-Step Process
A visual process section that demystifies what working with SIMA looks like: contact, consultation, execution, completion. This reduces anxiety for clients who may be unfamiliar with hiring this type of service.

### Contact Form with Intent
The contact form includes a service type selector, so inquiries arrive pre-qualified. Data protection consent is built in for GDPR compliance. The form sits alongside full contact details (phone, email, address, business hours) for visitors who prefer direct communication.

### FAQ Section
Four expandable questions addressing common concerns — building trust by proactively answering what clients are thinking.

### Two-Language SEO
Full German and Hungarian versions with hreflang tags, localized URLs, and comprehensive Schema.org structured data — LocalBusiness, FAQPage, OfferCatalog, GeoCircle for the 50km service radius. This gives SIMA strong local search presence in both language markets.

## The Outcome

A website that does exactly what the client needed — nothing more, nothing less. Clean, professional, trustworthy. Visitors land on the page and within seconds understand what SIMA offers, why they're credible, and how to get in touch. The two-language SEO means the site captures both the German-speaking Austrian market and the Hungarian community looking for bilingual support.

Sometimes the best solution is the simplest one, executed with precision.
