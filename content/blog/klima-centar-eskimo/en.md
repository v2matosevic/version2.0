# Klima Centar Eskimo: A Website Redesign That Boosted Speed by 150%

A slow website in the HVAC industry is worse than no website at all. When someone's air conditioning breaks in July, they're not waiting around for your page to load. They're clicking the next result.

Eskimo came to us with a site that didn't match the quality of their work. Here's what we did about it.

## The Client

Klima Centar Eskimo is an air conditioning company based in Croatia. They sell, install, and service HVAC systems for residential and commercial clients. Their offline reputation was solid. Repeat customers. Word-of-mouth referrals. The kind of business that earns trust through reliable work.

Their website? A different story.

## The Challenge

Eskimo's existing site had three problems working against it.

**Speed.** Pages loaded slowly enough to frustrate anyone looking for quick answers about HVAC services. In an industry where people search with urgency (broken AC in summer, heating failure in winter), slow load times mean lost customers.

**Design.** The site looked dated. Cluttered layouts, inconsistent styling, and a visual identity that didn't reflect the professionalism of the actual company. First impressions matter, and the site was making a poor one.

**SEO.** The site's content and metadata weren't structured for search visibility. Potential customers searching for HVAC services in their area weren't finding Eskimo. That's traffic and revenue left on the table.

## What We Built

We stripped everything back and rebuilt from scratch. No templates. No page builders. Clean, custom code designed for speed and clarity.

**Design.** The new site uses a clean white and light blue color palette that feels fresh and appropriate for an HVAC brand. Navigation is simple and intuitive. Every page has a clear purpose and a visible next step, whether that's requesting a quote, viewing product lines, or calling directly.

**Performance.** This was the big win. After our optimization work, the site's loading speed improved by 150%. We compressed images, minimized code, eliminated unnecessary scripts, and built the pages to load only what they need. [Page speed](/blog/how-to-optimize-web-page-speed/) improvements at this level directly affect both rankings and conversion rates.

**SEO.** We restructured all content with proper heading hierarchy, keyword-targeted meta tags, and clean URL structures. We wrote new copy for service pages that targets the specific terms potential customers use when searching for HVAC services in Croatia. Combined with the speed improvements, Eskimo started showing up for searches they'd never appeared in before.

**Mobile experience.** The entire site was built [mobile-first](/blog/importance-of-responsive-design-on-mobile-devices/). Someone searching "AC repair near me" on their phone at 2 AM gets a fast, usable site with a tap-to-call button front and center.

## The Result

After launch, Eskimo saw a significant increase in website traffic. New visitors from organic search started coming in for HVAC-related queries they hadn't ranked for previously. The site now matches the professionalism of their actual service: fast, clean, and easy to navigate.

The speed improvement alone was worth the investment. Faster load times mean better Google rankings, lower bounce rates, and more visitors who actually stick around long enough to convert.
