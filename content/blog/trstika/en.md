# Trstika: From Zero Online Presence to Generating Leads

Not every website needs to be complex. Sometimes the best thing you can do is present your services clearly, look professional, and make it dead simple for people to contact you. That's exactly what Trstika needed.

## The Client

Trstika is a service-based business in Croatia that needed to establish a credible online presence. They offer a range of services and wanted a website that could present all of them in a clear, organized way while looking polished and professional.

## The Challenge

Trstika had no website. For a service business, that's a serious problem. When potential customers search for what you offer and find nothing, they move on to someone who does show up.

The requirements were clear:

- A site that loads fast and looks professional
- Clear presentation of all services with supporting imagery
- Mobile-friendly design (most local searches happen on phones)
- [SEO fundamentals](/services/seo/) baked in from day one
- An easy path for visitors to get in touch

No unnecessary complexity. No feature bloat. Just a clean presentation of who they are and what they do.

## What We Built

We [designed and developed a website](/services/web-design/) focused on clarity and usability. The layout walks visitors through Trstika's services with clean sections and an image gallery showcasing their work. Navigation is straightforward. No deep menus, no confusion.

### Key Features

- **Responsive design** that works on phones, tablets, and desktops without compromise
- **Image gallery** showcasing completed projects, giving visitors proof of quality
- **On-page SEO** including proper heading structure, meta tags, and local keyword targeting
- **Analytics tracking** so Trstika can see exactly who's visiting and which content performs best
- **Contact integration** making it easy for visitors to reach out directly

Everything was built with the customer in mind. A visitor should be able to land on the site, understand what Trstika offers, see proof of their work, and get in touch. All within a couple of minutes.

## The Result

Trstika went from zero online presence to a professional website that brings in inquiries. The site ranks for relevant local searches, and the analytics show steady organic traffic growth since launch.

For a business that relied entirely on word of mouth before, having a website that generates its own leads is a meaningful shift.

> We couldn't be happier with the result and would recommend Version2 to anyone needing web design services.
> -- **Trstika**

## What This Project Shows

You don't always need bells and whistles. A well-designed, fast, SEO-optimized website that clearly presents your services will outperform a complex site that confuses visitors. The fundamentals matter more than the features.
