# Home Interijeri: A Website for Custom Furniture Craftsmanship

Custom furniture is a visual business. If your website can't show what you make in a way that feels premium, you're losing clients before they ever pick up the phone.

## The Client

Home Interijeri is a Croatian company specializing in bespoke furniture for homes and offices. Their work ranges from luxury residential interiors to modern commercial spaces. Quality craftsmanship is their thing. They needed a website that communicated that instantly.

## The Challenge

Home Interijeri had no meaningful online presence. For a company whose entire value proposition is visual, that's a serious problem. Potential clients had no way to browse their work, understand their process, or get in touch easily. They needed a site that let their portfolio do the talking.

## What We Built

We developed a clean, modern [website](/services/web-design/) designed around visual impact. The layout prioritizes large imagery so visitors can see the quality of the furniture and interiors immediately.

Navigation is straightforward. Visitors can browse through different categories, from home decor to office spaces, without getting lost in menus. The site works perfectly across all devices. On a phone, on a tablet, on a desktop monitor. The experience stays consistent.

We also set up analytics tracking and [SEO fundamentals](/services/seo/) so Home Interijeri can understand where their traffic comes from and how visitors interact with the site. Proper image optimization was critical here. Large, high-quality furniture photos need to look stunning without killing load times.

## The Result

Home Interijeri went from invisible online to having a professional digital presence that matches the quality of their physical work. The site serves as both a portfolio and a lead generation tool, bringing in inquiries from people who've already seen the work and are ready to talk.
