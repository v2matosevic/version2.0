A Zagreb-based artisanal shop increased conversion rates from 1.2% to 4.1% by reallocating 60% of their Google Ads budget to Facebook and Instagram with optimized creative content and retargeting strategies, demonstrating that platform selection significantly impacts campaign performance.

#### Why Choosing Wrong Platforms Costs Money

The fundamental error businesses make involves spreading budgets across multiple platforms rather than mastering one or two. Key considerations include:

- **Audience variation**: Different platforms attract different user behaviors and demographics
- **Content customization requirements**: Creative approaches must adapt to each platform's format and culture
- **Algorithm differences**: Success requires understanding platform-specific ranking mechanisms

#### Website Readiness Prerequisites

Before launching paid campaigns, ensure:

- **Loading speed**: Pages should load under 3 seconds; each additional second costs approximately 7% in conversions
- **Mobile optimization**: 60% of Croatian traffic originates from mobile devices
- **Clear call-to-action**: Generic phrases underperform compared to specific directives like "Book a free consultation"

#### Platform-Specific Recommendations

**Facebook & Instagram**
- Best for: Local businesses, e-commerce, services targeting audiences over 30
- Advantages: Precise geographic targeting, retargeting capabilities
- Example result: Fitness studio acquiring 15-20 members monthly at €28 per acquisition

**LinkedIn**
- Best for: B2B services, consultants, professional education
- Advantages: Quality audience with higher purchasing power, reduced competition
- Example result: IT consultant generating 3-5 quality leads monthly, averaging €5,000 per contract

**TikTok & YouTube Shorts**
- Best for: Youth audiences, entertainment, creative viral content
- Advantages: Organic reach remains viable; creative video content receives platform rewards

**Google Ads**
- Best for: Services people actively search for (plumbers, dentists, lawyers)
- Advantages: Captures high-intent audiences already seeking solutions
