# Why Hostinger Is the WordPress Hosting We Actually Recommend

Your hosting is the foundation your website sits on. Pick a bad one and you get downtime, slow pages, and support tickets that vanish into a void. Pick a good one and you forget it exists. That is the goal.

We have built and managed WordPress sites on a lot of hosting platforms over the years. [Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/?REFERRALCODE=1MARKO91) is the one we keep coming back to. Not because it is perfect at everything, but because it nails the things that actually matter for most projects.

## Uptime That Does Not Make You Nervous

[Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/?REFERRALCODE=1MARKO91) guarantees 99.9% uptime. In real-world terms, that means your site stays online. No random outages at 2 AM. No panicked emails from clients asking why their homepage is blank.

This is not marketing fluff. Their infrastructure is solid, and we have monitored it ourselves across dozens of sites. If your site goes down, your visitors leave. Your rankings drop. Your revenue takes a hit. Uptime is not optional.

## Global CDN Means Faster Load Times

A site hosted on a single server in one country will load slowly for visitors everywhere else. That is just physics. [Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/?REFERRALCODE=1MARKO91) solves this with a global content delivery network that caches your site across multiple data centers worldwide.

The result: your pages load fast regardless of where your audience is browsing from. This matters more than most people realize. Google uses page speed as a ranking factor. Visitors bounce from slow sites. Every millisecond counts. If you want to dig deeper into speed optimization, check out our guide on [how to optimize your website speed](/blog/how-to-optimize-web-page-speed/).

## WordPress Hosting That Actually Works

Since WordPress powers a massive chunk of the web, you would think every host would optimize for it. They do not. Many hosts treat WordPress as an afterthought.

[Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/?REFERRALCODE=1MARKO91) offers one-click WordPress installation, pre-configured caching, and server-level optimizations built for the platform. You do not need to spend hours tweaking PHP settings or installing caching plugins just to get decent performance.

For teams already running WordPress, this means less time fighting your hosting and more time building. If you are new to WordPress, read our [beginner's guide to WordPress web design](/blog/web-design-using-wordpress-quick-guide-for-beginners/) to get started the right way.

Cheap hosting is everywhere. The problem is that cheap usually means slow, unreliable, or both. [Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/?REFERRALCODE=1MARKO91) sits in a sweet spot. The plans are affordable without cutting corners on the things that matter.

## Shared vs. VPS: Which Plan Do You Need?

For most small business websites and blogs, Hostinger's shared plans are fine. They are budget-friendly and handle moderate traffic without issues.

If you are running a high-traffic site, an e-commerce store, or anything that needs dedicated resources, move up to their VPS plans. The difference is noticeable: better performance, more control, and stronger security isolation.
