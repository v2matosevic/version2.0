# Aquaart Bathrooms: Redesigning a Premium Bathroom Brand Online

When you sell premium bathroom products, your website can't look like it was built in 2015. Aquaart Bathrooms needed a site that matched the quality of what they actually sell.

## The Client

Aquaart Bathrooms is a premium bathroom product brand. Their catalog includes high-end fixtures, tiles, and accessories for customers who care about design as much as function.

## The Challenge

Their existing website was underperforming across the board. Page speed sat around 70%. Product presentation didn't reflect the quality of the brand. And there was no easy way for customers to find where to actually buy the products in person.

The site also needed to serve multiple markets, which meant proper multilingual support. Not just translated text slapped onto the same layout, but a considered localization approach.

## What We Built

We did a full [redesign](/services/web-design/) from the ground up. The new site puts products in the spotlight with clean layouts and high-quality imagery. Every page is built for speed. We pushed performance well above the original 70% baseline.

The [SEO work](/services/seo/) went beyond the basics. We restructured the site architecture, optimized metadata, and improved internal linking. Search visibility climbed across all target markets.

One of the most useful features is the product locator. Customers can search for a specific product and see exactly which retail locations carry it. This bridges the gap between browsing online and buying offline.

We also wrote fresh copy throughout the site. Clear, benefit-focused language that speaks to homeowners and designers alike.

## The Result

Aquaart Bathrooms now has a site that reflects the brand properly. Faster load times, better search rankings, multilingual reach, and a product locator that genuinely helps customers make purchasing decisions.
