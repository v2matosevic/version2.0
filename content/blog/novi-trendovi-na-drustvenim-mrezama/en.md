With the constant development of technology, our social media habits are constantly changing. In 2024, we are witnessing a series of new trends that shape the way we communicate, share content and connect with others. With increasing interactivity and personalization, platforms like Facebook, Instagram and TikTok are becoming increasingly sophisticated. Our experience on these networks is now richer because of new tools and features that encourage us to express ourselves more creatively.

Our virtual behavior is increasingly directed towards building strong communities and interests. The integration of AR (augmented reality) technology and VR (virtual reality) experiences is not only something we follow with interest, but actively shapes our daily interaction on these platforms. Additionally, it is becoming apparent that brands that are aware of the importance of user engagement on social media are using advanced strategies to create a deeper relationship with their audience.

If you're looking for ways to make the most of these new social media opportunities, there are services that can help you understand and apply the new trends. Social media management experts can be your allies in a world where we are growing day by day and learning how to be relevant and visible. They can help us direct our strategy to maximize the power of platforms like Facebook, Instagram and TikTok.

## New functions and platforms

In 2024, we are witnessing significant progress in social networks through the introduction of new functionalities and the development of fresh platforms that change the way we connect and communicate.

### Development of AR and VR

**Extended (AR)** and **virtual reality (VR)** have become crucial in our daily virtual socializing. Features such as AR filters and VR spaces allow us to have a more interactive experience.

- **AR Filters**: We use them for various fun effects on selfies and in live chats.
- **VR Spaces**: We gather in virtual rooms for socializing, games and events that imitate real experiences.

### Independent social networks

With new **independent social networks** every day we meet new communities and ways to connect that target specific interests.

- **Hobby Networks**: Specialized for lovers of various activities – from cooking to hiking.
- **Professional Gatherings**: Platforms intended to connect experts from specific industries.

### E-commerce integration

**E-commerce** is closely intertwined with our experience on social networks, making shopping simpler and more direct.

- **Buy now**: With the 'Buy now' button, we easily go from viewing the product to buying it.
- **Virtual Trial Rooms**: We try on clothes and accessories through AR technology before making a purchase decision.

## Changes in user behavior

In 2024, we are witnessing how our habits on social networks are changing significantly. We present two distinct trends in user behavior that shape our digital everyday life.

### Digital detox

We see a growing trend **digital detox** among users of social networks. Worried about the negative effects of overuse, many of us opt for periodic screen breaks. We have compiled a short list of strategies we adopt:

- Limiting the daily time spent on social networks.
- Implementation of applications that monitor and limit our use.
- Determining "digital-free" zones within our homes.

### Interactive content

On the other hand, we are seeing an increase in interactive content that encourages users to take action instead of passive consumption. These are some of the main features of such content:

- Use **polls**, quizzes and interactive stories that encourage engagement.
- Development of AR filters and VR experiences that transform the way we experience content.
- Focus on content creation that encourages users to discuss and participate in content creation.
