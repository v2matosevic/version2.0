# Klima Centar Traživuk: Building an Online Sales Channel for HVAC Equipment

Selling air conditioning equipment online requires more than a product catalog. Customers need to find the right unit, compare specs, understand what fits their space, and get in touch quickly. Traživuk needed a site that handled all of that without friction.

## The Client

Klima Centar Traživuk is an HVAC equipment supplier in Croatia. They carry a range of air conditioning and heating products for residential and commercial use. Their business was growing through traditional channels, but they lacked the one thing competitors were already using to capture new customers: a proper online presence with e-commerce capabilities.

## The Challenge

Traživuk had a dual requirement that many small businesses face. They needed a professional company website that established credibility and explained their services. And they needed a web shop that let customers browse products, compare specifications, and either purchase directly or request a custom quote.

These two goals often compete with each other. A corporate site prioritizes storytelling and trust. An e-commerce site prioritizes product discovery and fast checkout. The challenge was building one cohesive experience that did both jobs well, without either feeling like an afterthought.

They also wanted a platform that could grow with them. Starting with a focused catalog, with room to add products, categories, and content over time.

## What We Built

We designed and developed a complete [website with integrated e-commerce](/services/e-commerce/) functionality. The design uses a clean light blue and white palette, professional and appropriate for the HVAC industry while still feeling approachable.

**Product catalog with smart organization.** Products are organized into clear categories with filters that let customers narrow down by type, capacity, brand, and use case. Each product page includes full specifications, images, and pricing. Contractors browsing on their phone between jobs can find what they need as easily as a homeowner researching on a laptop.

**Company presence.** Beyond the shop, the site includes service descriptions, company information, and an about section that builds trust with first-time visitors. For an industry where installations cost thousands of euros, trust matters before anyone clicks "buy."

**Contact integration.** Not every HVAC sale is a simple add-to-cart transaction. Commercial projects and large installations require custom quotes. We built a prominent contact form and inquiry system so customers can reach out for personalized assistance without leaving the site.

**Testimonials section.** Real customer feedback, displayed prominently, to build credibility with new visitors. In a market where many competitors have no web presence at all, social proof is a differentiator.

**Blog for [SEO value](/services/seo/).** We set up a blog section for educational content. Guides on choosing the right AC unit, maintenance tips, energy efficiency comparisons. This content brings in organic search traffic from people researching before they buy, exactly the audience most likely to convert.

**Full responsiveness.** Every page, every product listing, every form works on every device. Mobile traffic accounts for the majority of visits, and the experience reflects that.

## The Result

Since launch, Traživuk has seen a clear increase in user engagement. The web shop gives them a sales channel that works 24/7, capturing inquiries and orders even outside business hours. The blog content is bringing in organic traffic for HVAC-related searches in their market, building a pipeline of customers who discover Traživuk through useful information.

The combined approach, professional company site plus functional web shop, means Traživuk competes online without losing the personal touch that built their reputation offline.
