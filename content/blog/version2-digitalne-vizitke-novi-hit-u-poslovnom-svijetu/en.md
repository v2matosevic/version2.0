You meet someone at a conference. You reach into your wallet and pull out a card. It looks like a regular business card. Same size. Same weight. But when you tap it against their phone, your entire professional profile appears on their screen.

That's a Version2 digital business card in action. And it's why over 486 professionals are already using one.

## What You Get

Let's be specific. No vague promises.

### A Physical NFC Card

This is an actual card. PVC material. Credit card size. Feels solid and professional in your hand. Inside, there's an NFC chip programmed to your unique profile URL.

NFC (Near Field Communication) is the same technology behind contactless payments. When you hold your card near a phone, the chip wakes up and tells the phone to open your profile page. Takes about two seconds.

No battery inside the card. No charging. No maintenance. The chip is powered by the phone's own electromagnetic field when they get close.

### QR Code Backup

Every Version2 card has a QR code printed on it. If someone's phone doesn't support NFC (rare, but it happens), they scan the QR code with their camera instead. Same destination. Same profile.

Two sharing methods on one card. The NFC handles most interactions. The QR code catches everything else. You're covered either way.

To understand more about QR codes and how they work, check out our guide on [how to make a free QR code](/blog/how-to-make-a-free-qr-code/).

### Custom Design

We don't hand you a white card with a default font. Every Version2 card is designed to match your brand.

Your colors. Your logo. Your typography. Your layout. Our design team works with you to create something that looks like it belongs in your wallet and represents your professional identity.

Whether you're a solo consultant or ordering 50 cards for your sales team, each card gets the design attention it deserves.

### Editable Online Profile

Your card points to a web profile that you control. Update it anytime:

- New phone number? Changed in seconds.
- Got promoted? Update your title.
- Launched a new website? Swap the link.
- Added a new social channel? Drop it in.

The physical card never needs replacing. It always points to your latest information.

This is the single biggest advantage over paper business cards. With paper, every change means a new print run. With Version2, every change is a quick edit on your profile.

### Analytics

See how your card is performing. How many taps this week. How many QR scans. When people are engaging with your profile.

This turns networking from a black box into something measurable. You handed out cards at a trade show last Tuesday. Did people actually look at your profile? Now you know.

## Why It's Catching On

486+ users didn't switch because digital cards are trendy. They switched because paper cards stopped making sense.

### The Paper Card Problem

You order 500 business cards. You hand them out at events and meetings. Three months later, you change jobs. Or get a new phone number. Or update your website.

Now you have 350 useless cards and need to order 500 new ones. This cycle repeats every time anything changes.

Worse, the cards you already handed out? They have your old information. People call your old number. They email your old address. They visit your old website. You look disorganized.

### The Digital Card Fix

One card. Updated in real time. Always current. Always ready.

The person you met six months ago still has your latest info because the card pointed to a URL, not static text. When they pull up your saved contact, the phone number is right. The email is right. The job title is right.

You never run out of cards because you only need one. You never reprint because nothing is printed except the design.
