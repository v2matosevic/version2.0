Monster Kebab, a local Zadar establishment known for quality kebabs, partnered with Version2 to strengthen its digital presence. The collaboration transformed the business from minimal online visibility to a recognized brand across social platforms.

#### Social Media Management & Optimization

Version2 implemented comprehensive social network management across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and Google Maps. The agency built an engaged audience base and enhanced visual content quality through professional photography and videography. Strategic Google Maps optimization ensured Monster Kebab appeared prominently in local searches, becoming the preferred choice for both residents and tourists seeking kebab options online.

#### Paid Advertising Campaign

To amplify visibility, Version2 launched targeted PPC advertising featuring high-quality product imagery and videos. The campaign strategy focused on brand strengthening and expanding Monster Kebab's reach beyond Zadar into broader online markets.

#### Results

The partnership delivered measurable growth in social following and online visibility. Monster Kebab evolved from a local favorite into a recognized online presence, with analytics demonstrating the effectiveness of the integrated digital strategy.
