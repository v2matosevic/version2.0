# What is digital marketing and how is it used?

Perhaps you have wondered why some brands simply "explode" on the Internet, while others barely survive? The secret is in digital marketing. But what exactly is it and how can you use it for your success?

## What is digital marketing?

Digital marketing is a set of strategies and techniques that use digital channels to promote products or services. Instead of traditional methods such as television commercials or flyers, digital marketing uses the Internet, social networks, email and mobile applications to reach audiences.

This type of marketing has become inevitable because people spend most of their time online today. According to [the latest data](https://datareportal.com/reports/digital-2024-croatia), the average Croatian spends more than 6 hours a day online. This means that the digital space is the right place to connect with potential customers.

## Main components of digital marketing

**SEO optimization** is the foundation of a successful online presence. SEO optimization helps your website appear at the top of Google results when people search for your services.

**Social media marketing** enables you to communicate directly with the audience. Social media marketing can dramatically increase your brand recognition and generate new clients.

**Email marketing** remains one of the most profitable channels. According to [HubSpot Research](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/email-marketing-stats), for every euro invested in email marketing, you can expect a return of 38 euros – a pretty good ratio, right?

**PPC advertising** through Google Ads or Facebook ads allows you to get quick results. You only pay when someone clicks on your ad, which makes this approach very controlled.

## How is digital marketing used in practice?

Successful digital marketing starts with understanding your target audience. You need to know who your ideal customers are, where they spend their time online, and what motivates them to buy.

Then you create content that will attract attention. Blogging has become crucial for attracting an organic audience. Quality content positions you as an expert in your field and builds trust with potential clients.

Social media is used to build a community around your brand. Instagram strategies can significantly expand your reach, especially if you are targeting a younger demographic.

## Advantages of digital marketing

The most important advantage is measurability. Unlike traditional marketing, you can see exactly how many people saw your ad, clicked on it, and purchased your product. [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/) allows detailed monitoring of all these metrics in real time.

Digital marketing also allows for precise targeting. You can show ads only to people of a certain age, location, or interests. This means you're not wasting money on people who are unlikely to buy your product.

Flexibility is another big advantage. If a campaign isn't working, you can adjust or stop it in real time. This is simply not possible with traditional advertising.

## Challenges of digital marketing

The digital space is becoming increasingly saturated. Millions of pieces of content are published every day, making it increasingly difficult to stand out. It takes creativity and persistence to break through the noise.

Social media algorithms are constantly changing, which can affect the reach of your content. What worked last month may not work today.

Also, digital marketing requires continuous efforts. It is not a "set it and forget it" strategy. You need to regularly create content, analyze results, and adjust your approach.

## How to start with digital marketing?

Start with clearly defined goals. Do you want to increase sales, brand awareness, or generate new leads? Your strategy will depend on what you want to achieve.

Then choose the channels that best suit your audience. If your customers use LinkedIn, focus there. If they are active on TikTok, that is your destination.

Measure results from the start. Use Google Analytics to track website traffic and social media tools to track engagement. Data will tell you what's working and what's not.

For those who feel overwhelmed, there are digital marketing agencies who can take on the burden and ensure professional results.

Digital marketing is not just a trend - it is the future of communication with customers. Those who adopt it now will have an advantage over the competition. The question is not whether you should engage in digital marketing, but how to best use it for your success.
