The advent of social networks has revolutionized the way companies promote their products and services. Ever since social media became popular, marketers have realized that it is an ideal opportunity to target their audience and increase sales. However, many people wonder when exactly the era of social media in marketing began.

Although social media was not intended for marketing purposes, marketers quickly realized its potential. The first attempts at social media marketing began in the late 90s and early 2000s, when companies started using blogs and forums to promote their products and services. However, the real revolution happened with the appearance of Facebook in 2004, which became the most popular social network in the world and opened the door to new marketing opportunities.

Since then, social media has become an indispensable part of the marketing strategies of companies around the world. Today, there are several social networks that are used for marketing purposes, including Facebook, Instagram, Twitter and LinkedIn. An increasing number of companies are recognizing the importance of social media in promoting and selling their products and services, which means that social media will continue to be a key part of marketing strategies in the future.

### The beginnings of social networks and marketing

#### The first social network

The first social network to appear was Six Degrees, launched in 1997. Users could create a profile and connect with other users. However, Six Degrees failed to gain much popularity and was closed in 2001.

After that, other social networks like Friendster, MySpace and LinkedIn appeared, but they also had their drawbacks. Only the appearance of Facebook in 2004 marked the beginning of social networks as we know them today.

#### Early forms of online advertising

Online advertising appeared back in the 1990s, but was limited to displaying banners on web pages. Only later did new forms of advertising appear, such as Google AdWords and Facebook Ads.

In the beginning, social networks were not popular as an advertising channel, but with the growth of the number of users, social marketing began to develop. Today, social networks are one of the most important channels for advertising and promoting products and services.

### Development and evolution

#### The rise of Facebook and Twitter

Social media marketing began with the development of social networks such as Facebook and Twitter. Facebook was launched in 2004, and Twitter in 2006. These networks have enabled companies to reach their target groups in a new way. Companies have started publishing content on their sites to attract the attention of social media users.

Posting content on social networks has become an important means of increasing brand awareness and user engagement. Companies have started using social networks to advertise their products and services. In addition, Facebook and Twitter have allowed businesses to communicate directly with users, leading to improved user experience.

#### The era of influencers and content marketing

After the rise of Facebook and Twitter, came the emergence of influencers and content marketing. Influencers have become popular on social media, and companies have started working with them to promote their products and services. Influencers have become an important part of a company's marketing strategy, as they have a great impact on their followers.

Content marketing became popular because companies realized that users don't just want to see ads. Instead, they want to see value in the content shared on social networks. Companies started to create content that would be interesting and useful for their target group.

In short, social media marketing began with the development of social networks such as Facebook and Twitter. After that, there was the emergence of influencers and content marketing, which led to the improvement of the company's marketing strategy.
