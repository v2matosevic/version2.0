Last month, the team from NK Abeceda approached us with a clear goal – they needed a video that would attract new young players to their club. Not just any video, but something that would immediately show parents the atmosphere, the quality of training, and most importantly – how happy the kids are on the field.

#### What we recorded for NK Abeceda

We spent a day at their training ground filming young players doing drills. We filmed short interviews with the players who shared why they love this club, and using a drone, we captured spectacular aerial footage showing the quality of the pitch and infrastructure. We wanted to capture the real atmosphere – the kind that parents usually only see when they bring their child to training.

The result is a one-minute promo video which tells the complete story. You can see the professionalism of the coaches, the quality of the infrastructure and what was most important for us – how much the children enjoy football. Because at the end of the day, that's what parents want for their children.

#### Why video works for sports clubs

Video content on social networks increases engagement by as much as 1200% compared to regular photos. When a club posts a photo from training, someone might scroll through it. But when they post a video where you can see the atmosphere and hear the kids laughing, it simply attracts attention.

NK Abeceda realized this in time. Instead of relying solely on word-of-mouth recommendations, they decided to show what they offer through professional video production.
