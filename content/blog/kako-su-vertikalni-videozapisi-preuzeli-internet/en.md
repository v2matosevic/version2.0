# How vertical videos took over the internet

Few trends have transformed content like the rise of vertical video. Gone are the days when normal wide videos dominated. Today, thanks to platforms like TikTok and Instagram, vertical videos have become the norm, influencing not only user behavior but also the way professionals approach video production.

## Shift from horizontal to vertical recording

Historically, horizontal videos have dominated our screens, from television shows to YouTube streams. However, the smartphone revolution has changed the game. Since users generally hold their devices upright, platforms saw an opportunity to cater to this behavior. Applications such as Snapchat, TikTok and Instagram have made vertical viewing an integral part of their design, encouraging creators to produce content specifically tailored for vertical audiences.

## Why is vertical video more attractive to users?

Vertical videos also offer a different user experience. Taking up the entire screen, they draw viewers in, keeping distractions to a minimum. This format is especially effective for short, impactful content where creators only have a few seconds to grab attention. Brands and experts for video production they took note and optimized their content strategies to prioritize vertical video and maximize user engagement.

## Impact on professional video production and editing

With the increasing demand for vertical content, there has been a significant shift in video production and video montage. Filmmakers, who once trained their eyes for horizontal shots, now consider vertical composition, ensuring that key elements fit within a confined space. Moreover, techniques video editing have evolved to adapt to this format, with editors introducing dynamic cuts, zooms and transitions to cater for vertical viewership.

## Adapting to the vertical video revolution

Brands that don't want to adapt risk being left behind. It's not just about reshaping existing content; it's about conceptualizing, shooting and editing with the vertical in mind. This may seem daunting, but with the right approach and expertise in video production, businesses can create engaging content that resonates with their target audience. Platforms such as Instagram and TikTok offers a huge, engaged user base eager for fresh content, presenting the brand with an unparalleled opportunity to connect.

## Conclusion

The rise of vertical video highlights the dynamic nature of the digital landscape. As user behavior evolves, so must content strategies. With platforms like TikTok and Instagram at the forefront of this revolution, brands need to recognize the power of vertical video and capitalize on its potential.
