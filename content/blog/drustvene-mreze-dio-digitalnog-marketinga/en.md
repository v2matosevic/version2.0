Social networks have become an integral part of our lives. Almost everyone with internet access uses social media to connect with other people, share content and follow trends. But are social networks part of digital marketing?

We think the answer is yes. Social networks have become a key tool for marketing campaigns, as they allow companies to reach a large number of people in one place. Also, social media allows businesses to target specific groups of people based on their interests, demographics, and online behavior.

However, it is important to note that social media is not the only part of digital marketing. Digital marketing encompasses a wide range of tools and strategies that companies use to promote their products and services through digital channels. Social media is just one of many tools that businesses can use to achieve their marketing goals.

### Basics of digital marketing

#### Definition of digital marketing

As the name suggests, digital marketing encompasses all marketing activities that take place through digital channels. This includes websites, social networks, email marketing, SEO and PPC advertising and other forms of digital channels. The goal of digital marketing is to attract and engage the target audience and convert them into customers.

#### The role of social media

Social networks have become an indispensable part of digital marketing. They allow us to connect directly with our audience, create a community and build brand awareness. By using social networks, we can place targeted ads, monitor analytics and get feedback from the audience.

Social networks, or "Social Networks" as it is translated into Croatian, also allow us to connect with influencers and brand ambassadors and to create viral content. It is important to have a social media strategy that is relevant to your target audience and that is continuously adjusted in order to achieve digital marketing success.

### Social media strategies

As we have already discussed, social media is an important part of digital marketing. However, in order to achieve success in this area, it is necessary to establish a strategy that will help us achieve our marketing goals. In this section, we will discuss some key strategies that we can employ on social media.

#### Content and engagement

One of the most important strategies for social media is creating quality content and encouraging audience engagement. This means that we need to create content that is interesting, useful and relevant to our audience. Also, we need to encourage interaction with the audience, such as comments, shares and likes. This will help us increase our brand visibility and build a loyal audience.

#### Advertising on social networks

Social media advertising is another important strategy that we can employ. This allows us to target specific groups of people and show them relevant ads. For example, we can target people who are interested in our product or service, or people who live in a certain area. This helps us achieve higher conversion and ROI.

#### Measuring success

Finally, it is important to track and measure the success of our social media campaigns. This allows us to see what works and what doesn't, and adjust our strategy to achieve better results. Some of the key success indicators include follower count, audience engagement, conversions and ROI.

In short, creating quality content, advertising and measuring success are key strategies we can employ on social media to achieve our marketing goals.
