# LCL Optika: An E-Commerce Site for Handcrafted Eyewear

Handcrafted eyewear is a product you sell with your eyes. The website needs to feel as considered as the frames themselves. Off-the-shelf templates weren't going to cut it for LCL Optika.

## The Client

LCL Optika creates handcrafted eyewear, primarily sunglasses. Each pair is made with attention to detail that mass-market brands can't match. Their product is premium. They needed an online store that communicated that from the first click.

## The Challenge

LCL Optika wanted an e-commerce platform that balanced simplicity with sophistication. The shopping experience had to be effortless, but the design needed to reflect the craftsmanship behind each pair. They also needed flexible payment options to remove any barrier to purchase.

Selling handcrafted eyewear online comes with a specific UX problem: people want to see the product from every angle. They want to understand the materials, the finish, the weight. A product page that relies on a single flat photo doesn't do it justice. We needed a layout that gave each pair of glasses room to breathe.

## What We Built

We designed and developed a [web shop](/services/e-commerce/) that puts the eyewear front and center. The layout is intentionally minimal. No clutter, no distractions. Just beautiful product photography and clear paths to purchase.

### Product-First Design

Every product page was built to showcase the eyewear as the hero. Large, high-resolution images dominate the layout. Product details, materials, and sizing information sit below in a clean, scannable format. The color palette stays neutral so nothing competes with the frames themselves.

We also designed the collection browsing experience with filters for style, frame type, and price range. A visitor can go from "just browsing" to "I want these" in three clicks or fewer.

### Payment Flexibility

The checkout process supports multiple payment methods: Visa, MasterCard, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and Klarna for buy-now-pay-later. We wanted zero friction between "I love these" and "They're mine."

For a premium product with a higher price point, buy-now-pay-later options matter. Klarna integration alone opens up a segment of customers who might otherwise hesitate at the final step.

### Mobile-First Shopping

Eyewear shopping happens on phones. Instagram ad, tap, browse, buy. That's the path. The entire store is fully [responsive](/blog/importance-of-responsive-design-on-mobile-devices/), with touch-optimized navigation and a checkout flow designed for thumbs. Product images swipe naturally. The cart is accessible from every page. No pinching, no zooming, no frustration.

### SEO and Copywriting

We handled the [SEO strategy](/services/seo/) and copywriting for the entire site. Every product description was written to do two jobs: help the customer decide and help Google understand what the page is about. Page titles, meta descriptions, and collection pages target terms around handcrafted and designer eyewear.

The result is a site that doesn't just look good. It gets found.

## The Result

LCL Optika now has an online store that matches the quality of their product. The site feels premium without being pretentious, and the multiple payment options make buying easy for customers across Europe.

The combination of clean design, strong product photography, and smart payment integration gives LCL Optika a storefront that works as hard as the product itself.
