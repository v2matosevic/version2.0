# Right of Withdrawal & Refunds

**Last updated:** February 25, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This page explains your right of withdrawal from contracts concluded at a distance with VERSION2 j.d.o.o., the conditions under which refunds are granted, and the standard withdrawal form required by law. It applies to all purchases made through our website at www.version2.hr.

For full purchase terms, including payment, delivery, and liability, please refer to our [Terms and Conditions](/terms-and-conditions/).

## 2. Right of Withdrawal --- General Information

Under the Croatian Consumer Protection Act (*Zakon o zaštiti potrošača*, NN 19/22), which implements EU Directive 2011/83/EU on consumer rights, consumers who purchase goods or services online have the right to withdraw from a distance contract within **14 calendar days** without giving any reason and without incurring any costs other than those specified in this policy.

### Withdrawal Period

- **For goods:** The withdrawal period expires 14 days from the day on which you, or a third party other than the carrier indicated by you, acquires physical possession of the goods. Where multiple goods are ordered in one order and delivered separately, the period runs from the day on which you acquire physical possession of the last good.
- **For services:** The withdrawal period expires 14 days from the day of the conclusion of the contract.

To meet the withdrawal deadline, it is sufficient for you to send your communication concerning your exercise of the right of withdrawal before the withdrawal period has expired.

### How to Exercise the Right

To exercise the right of withdrawal, you must inform us of your decision to withdraw from the contract by an unequivocal statement. You may:

- **Send an email** to: info@version2.hr
- **Send a letter by post** to: VERSION2 j.d.o.o., Novigradska ulica 21, 23000 Zadar, Croatia

You may use the **Standard Withdrawal Form** provided in Section 5 below, but it is not mandatory. Any clear statement of your decision to withdraw from the contract is sufficient.

## 3. Exemptions from the Right of Withdrawal

The right of withdrawal does not apply in the following cases, as provided by law.

### 3.1 Digital Business Cards (Custom Goods)

Under **Article 79, paragraph 1, point 3** of the Croatian Consumer Protection Act (implementing Article 16(c) of EU Directive 2011/83/EU):

> The right of withdrawal does not apply to the supply of goods that are made to the consumer's specifications or are clearly personalized.

All Version2 digital business cards are custom-manufactured products. Each card is individually designed with the customer's branding --- including logo, colors, typography, and layout --- and programmed with the customer's unique profile data, including name, contact details, and links. Even when the customer selects a pre-designed template, the card is individually customized and personalized to their specifications before production begins.

Because each card is produced to the customer's individual specifications, **the right of withdrawal does not apply to digital business card orders once production has commenced.**

The customer is clearly informed of this exemption and expressly acknowledges it before completing the purchase.

### 3.2 Digital Services (Web Design, SEO, and Other Services)

Under **Article 79, paragraph 1, point 1** of the Croatian Consumer Protection Act (implementing Article 16(a) of EU Directive 2011/83/EU):

> The right of withdrawal does not apply to service contracts after the service has been fully performed, if the performance began with the consumer's prior express consent and their acknowledgment that they will lose their right of withdrawal once the contract has been fully performed.

For web development and other digital service contracts:

- **Before work begins:** You retain the full right of withdrawal during the 14-day withdrawal period, provided that you have not given express consent for work to begin.
- **When you approve project kickoff** --- by signing the project proposal or giving express written consent to begin work --- you consent to the start of performance and acknowledge that the right of withdrawal will progressively lapse as the service is performed.
- **Partial performance:** If you withdraw after performance has begun but before the service is fully completed, you are liable to pay an amount proportional to what has been provided up to the time you communicated your withdrawal, in comparison with the full scope of the contract.
- **Full performance:** Once the service has been fully performed with your prior express consent, the right of withdrawal no longer applies.

## 4. Effects of Withdrawal (Where Applicable)

If the right of withdrawal applies to your purchase and you exercise it within the statutory period, the following effects apply.

### Reimbursement

- We will reimburse **all payments** received from you, including the costs of delivery (except for supplementary costs arising from your choice of a type of delivery other than the least expensive standard delivery offered by us).
- Reimbursement will be made **without undue delay and in any event within 14 days** from the day on which we are informed of your decision to withdraw from the contract.
- We will use the **same means of payment** you used for the initial transaction, unless you have expressly agreed otherwise. In any event, you will not incur any fees as a result of the reimbursement.
- We may **withhold reimbursement** until we have received the goods back, or you have supplied evidence of having sent back the goods, whichever is the earliest.

### Returning Goods

- Send the goods back to: **VERSION2 j.d.o.o., Novigradska ulica 21, 23000 Zadar, Croatia**
- Without undue delay and **in any event not later than 14 days** from the day on which you communicate your withdrawal to us.
- The deadline is met if you send back the goods before the 14-day period has expired.
- **The direct cost of returning the goods is borne by you** (the consumer).
- You are **liable for any diminished value** of the goods resulting from the handling of the goods other than what is necessary to establish the nature, characteristics, and functioning of the goods.

## 6. Defective Products

Separate from the right of withdrawal, you are entitled to a remedy if you receive a defective product. This right exists under the statutory conformity guarantee and is independent of the withdrawal period.

### Digital Business Cards

If your digital business card has a manufacturing defect, including but not limited to a non-functional NFC chip, printing errors caused by us, incorrect data programming, or physical defects present upon delivery:

- **Notify us within 30 days of delivery** by email or phone.
- We will **replace the defective card at no additional cost** to you, including shipping.
- If replacement is not possible, we will issue a full refund.

The following are **not covered** as manufacturing defects:

- Physical damage caused after delivery (drops, bending, scratching, water exposure)
- Damage resulting from use inconsistent with the product's intended purpose
- Changes to your personal data or branding after the card was produced (these can be updated digitally at any time through your dashboard)

### How to Report a Defect

- **Email:** info@version2.hr
- **Phone:** +385 99 561 7706
- Please include your **order number** and a **description or photograph** of the defect.

We will acknowledge your report within 3 business days and provide a resolution within 14 days.

## 7. Refund Processing

When a refund is due under this policy:

- **Card payments (via Stripe):** Refunded to the original payment method. Please allow up to 14 days for the refund to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or card issuer.
- **Bank transfers:** Refunded to the bank account from which the payment was made, within 14 days of approval.
- **Other payment methods:** Refunded using the same means of payment used for the initial transaction, unless you expressly agree otherwise.

You will receive an email confirmation when the refund has been processed.
