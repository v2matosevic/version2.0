# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** February 25, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how VERSION2 j.d.o.o. ("we," "us," "our") uses them on [www.version2.hr](https://www.version2.hr), and how you can control them. This policy should be read alongside our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy/), which provides full details on how we process personal data.

This policy complies with the ePrivacy Directive (2002/58/EC) and the Croatian Electronic Communications Act (Zakon o elektroničkim komunikacijama, Art. 104).

---

## 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They serve various purposes: some are essential for the website to function correctly, while others help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

Cookies can be "first-party" (set by the website you are visiting) or "third-party" (set by a different domain than the one you are visiting). They can also be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (remaining on your device for a defined period or until you delete them manually).

---

## 2. How We Use Cookies

We follow a consent-based approach to cookies:

- **Strictly necessary cookies** are always active because they are essential for the website to function. They do not require your consent.
- **All other cookies** (analytics and any future categories) are **off by default** and are only activated after you provide explicit opt-in consent through our cookie consent banner.

No non-essential cookies are set, and no tracking occurs, until you make an active choice to allow them.

---

## 3. Cookie Consent

We use a custom-built cookie consent banner that appears when you first visit our website. You have three options:

- **Accept all** — enables all cookie categories
- **Reject all non-essential** — only strictly necessary cookies remain active
- **Customize** — choose exactly which categories to allow

Your consent preferences are recorded and stored so that we do not need to ask you again on every visit. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the **cookie settings** link in the website footer. Updating your preferences takes effect immediately.

## 4. Cookie Categories

### Category 1: Strictly Necessary

These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of our website. They enable core features such as session management, language preferences, and consent storage. Because the website cannot function properly without them, these cookies **cannot be disabled**.

**Legal basis:** Exempt from consent requirement under Art. 5(3) of the ePrivacy Directive — strictly necessary for the provision of a service explicitly requested by the user.

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `cookie_consent` | version2.hr | Stores your cookie consent preferences (which categories you accepted or rejected) | First-party, persistent | 1 year |
| `session_id` | version2.hr | Maintains your browsing session for continuity as you navigate between pages | First-party, session | Session (deleted when browser closes) |
| `lang` | version2.hr | Stores your selected language preference so the site displays in the correct language on return visits | First-party, persistent | 1 year |

### Category 2: Analytics

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting information about which pages are visited, how long sessions last, and where visitors come from. This data helps us improve the website's structure, content, and performance.

**These cookies are only active with your explicit consent.**

| Cookie Name | Provider | Purpose | Type | Duration |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes unique users by assigning a randomly generated client identifier. Used to calculate visitor, session, and campaign data for site analytics reports. | First-party, persistent | 2 years* |
| `_ga_[container-id]` | Google Analytics | Maintains session state and tracks which pages a user visits within a single session | First-party, persistent | 2 years* |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics | Distinguishes users for short-term analysis, allowing us to identify unique visitors within a 24-hour window | First-party, persistent | 24 hours |

*Note: While the cookie expiration is set to 2 years by Google, most modern browsers (including Safari and Firefox) limit first-party cookie lifetimes to approximately 400 days or less through Intelligent Tracking Prevention (ITP) and similar mechanisms.

**Backend analytics (no cookies):** We also collect anonymized page view and interaction data through our own backend analytics system. This system operates server-side and **does not use cookies**. It collects no personal identifiers. See section 2(d) of our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy/) for details.

### Category 3: Marketing

We do **not** currently use any marketing or advertising cookies. No tracking pixels, retargeting scripts, or ad-network cookies are present on our website.

If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated before any marketing cookies are introduced, and your explicit consent will be required before any such cookies are set.

## 6. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in two ways:

### Via Our Consent Banner

Click the **cookie settings** link in our website footer at any time to review and change your cookie preferences. Changes take effect immediately. You can switch analytics cookies on or off whenever you choose.

### Via Your Browser Settings

All major browsers allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies through their settings. You can typically find these options under "Privacy," "Security," or "Cookies" in your browser's settings menu:

- **Google Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Mozilla Firefox:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Apple Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Microsoft Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Consult your browser's help documentation for detailed instructions.

**Note:** Clearing your browser cookies will also delete your consent preferences stored in the `cookie_consent` cookie. This means you will see the consent banner again on your next visit and will need to make your choices again.

## 7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

| Cookie Category | Impact of Disabling |
|---|---|
| **Strictly necessary** | These cookies cannot be disabled. Without them, the website cannot function correctly — sessions would not persist, language preferences would not be saved, and consent choices could not be recorded. |
| **Analytics** | Disabling analytics cookies means we will not collect data about your visit through Google Analytics. This has **no impact on your browsing experience** — the website will function exactly the same way. It simply means your visit will not be included in our aggregated usage statistics. |
