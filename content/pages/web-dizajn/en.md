# Your website is not a brochure. It's a product.

We build custom websites from scratch. No templates. No WordPress. No page builders. Clean code that does exactly what your business needs and nothing it doesn't.

## What You Get

Every project ships with:

- **Responsive on every device.** Not "mobile-friendly." Designed for phones first, then scaled up.
- **Fast load times.** Optimized code, not a bloated page builder hiding behind a caching plugin.
- **SEO-ready structure.** Proper headings, meta tags, schema markup, sitemap. Built for search engines from day one.
- **Content you can update yourself.** We build a content management system so you don't need a developer every time you want to change a headline.
- **Multi-language support.** Croatian, English, German, or whatever your market needs. Proper localization, not a Google Translate widget.
- **Analytics integration.** Know who visits, what they click, and where they drop off.

## Our Stack

Next.js. React. TypeScript. Tailwind CSS. Node.js.

We pick the right tool for the project. Not the trendy one. These are the technologies that let us build fast, maintain easily, and ship sites that score 90+ on every Lighthouse metric.

## The Process

### 1. Discovery

We learn your business, your goals, your audience. You tell us what success looks like. We figure out how to get there.

### 2. Design

Wireframes first, then full designs. You see everything before we write a line of code. We revise until it's right.

### 3. Build

Custom code, built by our team. No outsourcing. No freelancers. Every line written in-house.

### 4. Test

Every device. Every browser. Every screen size. We break things on purpose so your visitors never have to.

### 5. Launch

We handle deployment, DNS, SSL, hosting setup. You don't touch a terminal. We make sure everything works in production, not just on our machines.

### 6. Support

The project doesn't end at launch. We stick around for fixes, updates, and whatever comes next.

## Questions We Get Asked

### How long does it take?

4 to 8 weeks from kickoff to launch. Depends on scope, content readiness, and feedback speed. Larger projects with complex features or many pages can take longer. We'll give you a timeline before we start.

### What do I need to provide?

Content, branding assets (logo, colors, fonts if you have them), and clear goals. Tell us what your website needs to do and who it's for. We handle everything else.

### Do you do WordPress?

We maintain existing WordPress sites. New projects are built custom. If you have a WordPress site that needs updates or fixes, we can help. If you're starting fresh, custom is the better path.

### What about hosting?

We set up and manage hosting as part of the project. You don't need to worry about servers, uptime, or SSL certificates. It's included.
