# Not Shopify. Not WooCommerce. Yours.

Every Shopify store looks like a Shopify store. Every WooCommerce site is one plugin update away from breaking. You deserve better.

We build online stores from scratch. You own the code. You own the data. No monthly platform fees. No limitations someone else decided for you.

## What We Build

### Product Catalogs

Filtering, sorting, search. Categories, variants, custom attributes. Products displayed the way your customers actually shop, not the way a template decided.

### Secure Checkout

SSL encryption, PCI compliance, multiple payment options. A checkout flow designed for your specific market and audience. Cart abandonment happens when checkout is confusing. Ours won't be.

### Inventory Management

Stock levels, low-stock alerts, variant tracking. Connected to your actual supply chain. Update from an admin panel, a barcode scanner, or an API.

### Order Tracking

From purchase to delivery. Status updates for you and your customers. Automated email notifications at every step. Integration with shipping providers for real-time tracking.

### Customer Accounts

Order history, saved addresses, wishlists, reorder buttons. Return customers spend more. Give them a reason to come back.

### Multi-Currency and Multi-Language

Sell across borders. Automatic currency conversion, language switching, localized content. One store, multiple markets.

### Shipping Integrations

Calculate rates automatically. Print labels. Track packages. We connect to the carriers your customers expect.

## Payment Integrations

We integrate what your customers actually use.

**Stripe** for card payments worldwide. **PayPal** for buyers who prefer it. **Croatian payment gateways** for the local market, including Corvus Pay and WSPay.

No payment processor lock-in. Switch providers without rebuilding your store.

## Why Custom Over Shopify or WooCommerce

**No monthly platform fees.** Shopify charges you every month for the right to sell your own products. Our stores cost nothing to run beyond hosting.

**No plugin roulette.** WooCommerce stores depend on dozens of plugins from different developers. One bad update breaks everything. Our code is one codebase, maintained by one team.

**No performance ceiling.** Template stores slow down as you add products and traffic. Custom stores are built to handle your actual scale.

**Complete ownership.** Your store runs on your server. Your data stays in your database. No platform can change their terms and affect your business overnight.

**No design constraints.** Want a product page that works differently from every other store? That's the default when you build custom.

## The Tech Behind It

**Frontend:** Next.js and React. Fast page loads, smooth transitions, responsive on every screen.

**Backend:** Node.js with PostgreSQL. Your products, orders, and customer data in a database you control.

**Payments:** Stripe SDK, PayPal API, local gateway integrations. Tested, secure, PCI-compliant.

**Search:** Fast, typo-tolerant product search. Filters that work instantly.
