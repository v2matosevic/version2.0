# SEO is not magic. It's work.

We don't promise you page 1 in 30 days. Nobody honest does. What we do promise: a technically sound site, content structured for search, and rankings that improve because we put in the work.

## What We Do

### Technical SEO

Site speed, crawlability, schema markup, XML sitemaps, canonical tags, Core Web Vitals. The stuff search engines care about that most people never check. We fix the foundation so everything else has a chance to work.

### On-Page SEO

Content optimization, keyword placement, meta titles and descriptions, heading structure, image alt text, internal linking. Every page on your site should be working for you. Most aren't.

### Content Strategy

Blog planning, topic clusters, keyword research, content calendars. We figure out what your audience is searching for and help you create content that answers those questions better than anyone else.

### Local SEO

Google Business Profile optimization, local citations, review strategy, location-based keywords. If your customers are in your city, they should find you before they find your competitors.

## Our Approach

We start with an audit. A real one. Not a PDF generated by an automated tool.

Then we fix what's broken. Slow pages, missing meta tags, crawl errors, broken links, thin content. The basics that most sites get wrong.

Then we build what's missing. Content gaps, backlink opportunities, schema markup, topic authority. The work that takes months but compounds over time.

No black hat tricks. No link farms. No keyword stuffing. No shortcuts that get you penalized six months from now.

## What You Get

- **Monthly reports showing actual work done.** Not a dashboard full of charts you don't understand. A list of what we did, what changed, and what's next.

## Questions We Get Asked

### How long until I see results?

3 to 6 months for meaningful improvement. SEO is a long game. If someone tells you they can get you results in 30 days, they're either lying or doing something that will hurt you later.

The first month is mostly audit and fixes. Months two and three are content and optimization. By month four, you should start seeing movement. By month six, the data tells a clear story.

### Do you guarantee rankings?

No. Anyone who does is lying.

Google's algorithm considers hundreds of factors, many of which are outside anyone's control. What we guarantee is consistent, high-quality work. The kind that search engines reward over time.

### What tools do you use?

Ahrefs for backlink analysis and keyword research. Semrush for competitive analysis and position tracking. Google Search Console for direct data from Google. Screaming Frog for technical audits.

We use professional tools and we know how to read them. That's the part that matters.
