# We're Version2. We write code.

## Our Story

We started in September 2022 in Zadar, Croatia. A small team that builds things for the web.

The honest version? We started as a digital marketing company. Social media, photos, videos, the whole package. We did it. We were decent at it. But then we got honest with ourselves. What we're actually good at is building. Websites, apps, tools. The things that work long after the campaign ends.

So we stripped everything else away. No more photo shoots. No more social media calendars. Now we only do what we do best: write code and ship projects. Over 100 of them so far, for clients across Croatia and Europe. Every one built from scratch.

## What We Believe

**Templates look like templates.** That's why we don't use them. Every project starts with a blank file. Your business isn't generic. Your website shouldn't be either.

**Your website is not a brochure.** It's a product. It needs to load fast, work on every device, and actually do something useful for your business. We build it like one.

**Speed matters.** Not as a talking point. As a measurable outcome. Sub-second load times. Real performance scores. Your visitors don't wait around, and neither should your website.

**Complexity is the enemy.** Code should be clean enough that anyone can maintain it. If we build something only we can understand, we've failed.

## The Team

Marko Matosevic founded Version2 and still leads every project. The team is small by design. No middle management. No account managers. No outsourcing.

We don't have a sales department. The people you talk to are the people who build your project. When you send an email, a developer reads it. When you have a question about your site, you get an answer from someone who wrote the code.

100+ projects completed. 100+ clients. 5.0 star rating across 40+ reviews. Not because we're perfect. Because we pick projects we can do well, and then we do them well.
