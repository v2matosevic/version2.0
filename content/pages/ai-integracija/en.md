# AI that does something useful.

Half the internet just added a chatbot to their website and called it AI. We're not interested in that.

We build AI features that replace manual work, answer real questions, and make products smarter. Tools people actually use because they save time, not because they're novel.

## What We Build

### AI Chatbots Trained on Your Content

Not a generic chatbot that apologizes and suggests you email support. A chatbot trained on your actual content, products, pricing, and policies. It answers the questions your customers actually ask.

Customer support, sales qualification, FAQ handling, appointment booking. Available 24/7.

### Workflow Automation

Document processing that used to take someone three hours now takes three seconds. Invoice extraction, contract analysis, email classification, form processing. Point AI at the boring stuff so your team can do the interesting work.

### Smart Search and Recommendations

Search that understands what your users mean, not just what they type. Typo tolerance, synonym matching, contextual ranking. Product recommendations based on actual behavior, not "people also bought" guesswork.

### Content Generation Tools

First drafts, product descriptions, email templates. Not for replacing writers. For giving them a starting point instead of a blank page.

### Data Analysis and Reporting

Feed your data to a model and get plain-language insights back. Trend detection, anomaly alerts, summary reports. The analysis that used to require a data team.

## How It Works

We don't build AI from scratch. Nobody needs to train a model from zero when Claude and GPT already exist.

**For knowledge-based features**, we use RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation). Your content gets chunked, embedded, and stored. When a user asks a question, we retrieve the relevant pieces and generate an accurate answer. No hallucinations about things outside your data.

**For task-based features**, we use function calling and structured outputs. The AI doesn't just generate text. It triggers actions, fills forms, calls APIs, processes data.

**For specialized tasks**, we prompt-engineer for your specific domain. Medical, legal, real estate, e-commerce.

We integrate into your existing product or build the entire tool from scratch.

## Not Hype. Results.

Every company claims they're an "AI company" now. Most of them just added an API call and a chat bubble.

We don't add AI because it's fashionable. We add it when it solves a real problem faster or better than the alternative. If a dropdown menu does the job, we'll tell you to use a dropdown menu.

When AI is the right answer, we build it properly. Guardrails, fallbacks, monitoring, and a plan for when the model gets it wrong.

## The Models We Work With

**GPT** (OpenAI) for broad general knowledge and wide ecosystem support.

**Custom setups** when the task needs something specific. Local models for privacy-sensitive data. Specialized models for niche domains.

We're not locked to any provider. We use what works best for your use case.
